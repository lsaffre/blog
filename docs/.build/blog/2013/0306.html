Internal renames¶

As long as nobody looks at my work, I used my freedom and applied some internal renames:

  • Renamed django_site to djangosite. Because it’s clear enough without the underscore. Not sure whether I should also rename the official project from “django-site” to “djangosite”.
  • Converted djangosite.utils to a package.
  • Renamed djangosite.modeltools to djangosite.dbutils
  • lino.core.modeltools to lino.core.dbutils
  • lino.utils.sphinxconf to djangosite.utils.sphinxconf
  • djangosite.djangodoctests to djangosite.utils.doctests
  • lino.utils.rstgen to djangosite.utils.rstgen

What’s the difference between “tool” and “utility”?

Zwischenbemerkung: ich war wohl echt zufrieden mit Lino, wie gut er die obigen Änderungen mitgemacht hat.
