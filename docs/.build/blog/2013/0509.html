Polymorphism¶

The Cosi demo site had generated an interesting traceback which lead to another step in the evolution of lino.modlib.partners (which is currently being used by lino.projects.cosi). The challenge was to display a table with the ledger invoices of a Person or Organisation in a way that works with both possible choices for lino.ui.Site.partners_app_label. The new method lino.core.model.Model.get_typed_instance() is necessary for this.

New document Tested documents with a first section “Person #115 is not a Partner” which reproduced the traceback.

(Yes Polymorphism without MTI isn’t yet fully functional needs better documentation)

Renaming files from a bash command line¶

The easiest way to rename a series of files to make their names download-friendly (replace spaces by “_”, “ü” by “u”,...) is:

$ rename -n 's/ /_/g' *
$ rename -n 's/ü/u/g' *

(thanks to Mornedhel on http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1190887)
