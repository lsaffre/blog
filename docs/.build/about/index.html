About me¶

My name is Luc Saffre. I am an independent developer of free software. I was born and grew up in Eupen in Belgium. At the age of 30 I met an Estonian woman, we married, I moved to Estonia and hope to die there (not too soon). We have 2 daughters and live in a small village. We both work for our self-owned family company Rumma & Ko OÜ
